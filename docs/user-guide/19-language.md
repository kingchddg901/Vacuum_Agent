# 19 — Language

The card can speak your language. It ships in English plus German, French,
Spanish, Italian, Dutch, Portuguese, and Russian, and you can switch any time.

## Pick your language

Click the **globe** in the card header and choose a language. The change is
instant.

Your choice is **yours** — it applies only to your view, not to other people who
use the same dashboard, and it follows you across your devices (it's saved to
your Home Assistant account, not the browser).

Pick **Auto** to follow your Home Assistant language automatically.

!!! info "Drafts"
    Languages other than English are currently AI-assisted and marked *(draft)*
    until a native speaker has reviewed them — so they don't switch on
    automatically, only when you pick them from the globe. The wording will keep
    improving.

## Don't see your language?

Anyone can add one — a translation is just a file, no coding. If you'd like to
translate the card (or fix some wording), see
[Translating the card](../contributing/translating.md).
